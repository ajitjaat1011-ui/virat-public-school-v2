Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

  1. Home
  2. Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026

1. Who we are

Virat Public School ("the School", "we", "us") is a co-educational, RBSE-affiliated school located at Virat Nagar, Kotputli, Dist. Behror, Rajasthan — 303102. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information submitted through this website (the "Website").

2. What information we collect

We collect only the information you choose to provide through our website forms:

  • Admission Inquiry form: Parent name, student name, date of birth, class seeking admission to, parent phone number, optional email, address, optional message.
  • Contact form: Your name, email, optional phone, subject, message.
  • Result lookup: Roll number and date of birth. (We do not retain these inputs after the lookup completes.)

When you browse the website, basic technical information (browser type, pages visited) may be collected through standard server logs. If you accept analytics cookies, anonymized usage statistics may be collected through a privacy-friendly analytics tool.

3. What we do not collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit the following through this website:

  • Aadhaar numbers (in line with UIDAI guidelines)
  • Caste, religion, or income information
  • Payment information (online fee payment is not offered in Phase 1)
  • Biometric or health data
  • Personal phone numbers or emails of teaching staff

4. How we use your information

Information submitted through our forms is used only to:

  • Respond to admission inquiries (within 1 working day)
  • Respond to contact form messages (within 2 working days)
  • Verify result lookups
  • Improve the website based on anonymized usage data

5. Who we share information with

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share information only with:

  • School staff who need the information to respond to your enquiry
  • Service providers who help us operate the website (hosting, email delivery), bound by confidentiality
  • Government or regulatory authorities, if legally required

6. Cookies

The website sets only essential cookies required for the site to function (for example, the admin session cookie, which is used only when school staff log in to publish content). If you accept the optional analytics cookie, anonymized usage statistics are collected. You can decline analytics cookies without affecting your use of the website.

7. Data retention

  • Admission inquiries: retained for 24 months, then deleted.
  • Contact messages: retained for 12 months, then deleted.
  • Result lookup inputs: not retained after the lookup completes.

8. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal information you have submitted through this website
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to any legal retention requirements)

To exercise any of these rights, please email office@viratpublicschool.in with the subject line "Privacy Request". We will respond within 7 working days.

9. Children's information

The school does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 through this website. Admission inquiries are submitted by parents or guardians on behalf of their children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including HTTPS encryption, server-side input validation, and access controls on the admin dashboard. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when the policy was last revised. Material changes will be communicated through a notice on the website.

12. Contact
