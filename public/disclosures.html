7. Admission & Withdrawal Rules

Admission

  • Admissions are open to all children irrespective of caste, religion, or gender, subject to age eligibility.
  • 25% of seats in LKG and Class 1 are reserved under the Right to Education Act.
  • Age eligibility as on 31 March of the academic year: LKG — 3 yrs 6 months; UKG — 4 yrs 6 months; Class 1 — 5 yrs 6 months.
  • Admission is confirmed upon payment of the admission fee and submission of all required documents.

Withdrawal

  • Parents must submit a written withdrawal request to the school office at least one month in advance.
  • The Transfer Certificate is issued within 7 working days of receiving the written request, subject to all fees being cleared.
  • The admission fee is non-refundable. Tuition fees are refundable on a pro-rata basis only if the withdrawal request is submitted before 30 June of the academic year.
