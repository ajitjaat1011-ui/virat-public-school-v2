About Us

A school where every child is known, valued, and challenged.

For nearly two decades, Virat Public School has served the families of Virat Nagar, Kotputli, and the wider Behror region. We are a co-educational, English-medium institution affiliated to the Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education (RBSE), offering a structured learning journey from the first years of childhood through the senior secondary years.

Our approach pairs academic rigour — small class sizes, a committed faculty, and disciplined routines — with the warmth of a close-knit community where every child is known by name. We believe that strong character, clear thinking, and quiet confidence matter as much as marks.
