Infrastructure & Facilities

A campus built for serious learning, creative play, and the everyday care of children.

  1. Home
  2. Infrastructure

Spread across a 3.5-acre campus on the Kotputli–Behror road, our school offers well-maintained classrooms, purpose-built laboratories, generous outdoor space, and the everyday comforts — clean drinking water, hygienic washrooms, a medical room, and supervised transport — that allow children to learn well and parents to rest easy.

Our Campus

Facilities at a glance

📚

Smart Classrooms

42 well-ventilated, naturally lit classrooms across Pre-Primary, Primary, Secondary, and Senior Secondary wings. Each classroom is equipped with a smart board, projector, and age-appropriate furniture.

🔬

Science Laboratories

Three separate, fully equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, with a demonstration area, safety equipment, and trained lab assistants on duty during practical sessions.

💻

Computer Lab

A 40-station computer lab with high-speed internet, used for the computer-science curriculum from Class 3 onwards. Ratio of one computer per student during lab periods.

📖

Library

Over 6,000 volumes covering fiction, reference, encyclopedias, textbooks, periodicals, and a dedicated children's corner. Open throughout the school day with a full-time librarian.

🧮

Mathematics Lab

An activity-based mathematics lab with models, kits, and manipulatives that allow students from Class 1 to 10 to develop mathematical thinking through hands-on exploration.

⚽

Playground & Sports

A 1.5-acre playground with separate areas for football, kho-kho, kabaddi, and athletics. Indoor games room for table tennis, carrom, and chess. Annual Sports Meet held every December.

🎵

Music & Dance Room

A dedicated space for music, dance, and cultural activities. Equipped with instruments (harmonium, tabla, keyboard), mirrors, and a sound system for rehearsals and performances.

🎨

Art Room

A bright, well-lit art room with supplies for drawing, painting, craft, and clay work. Used for both curriculum art periods and after-school art club activities.

🚌

Transport Fleet

A fleet of 12 GPS-tracked buses covering Virat Nagar, Kotputli town, Behror, Srimadhopur Road, and surrounding villages. Each bus has a trained attendant, first-aid kit, and CCTV.

💧

Safe Drinking Water

RO-filtered drinking water available across the campus, tested quarterly for quality. Water coolers at every wing and on the playground.

🏥

Medical Room

A dedicated medical room with a full-time nurse, first-aid supplies, and a visiting paediatrician once a week. Emergency protocols in place; nearest hospital 5 minutes away.

🏛️

Auditorium

A 500-seat air-conditioned auditorium used for the annual day, inter-house competitions, guest lectures, and PTMs. Stage with professional lighting and sound.

Safety & security

The safety of children on our campus is non-negotiable. We have put in place the following measures, audited quarterly by the management committee:

  • ✓ 24×7 CCTV coverage across all classrooms, corridors, entry points, the playground, and inside all school buses. Footage retained for 30 days.
  • ✓ Single-entry campus with a manned security gate. All visitors must sign in, produce a valid ID, and wear a visitor badge. No entry during class hours without prior appointment.
  • ✓ Female attendant present in all primary-section classrooms and washrooms, in line with school-safety guidelines.
  • ✓ Fire safety — NOC obtained from the local fire department. Fire extinguishers, alarms, and marked evacuation routes on every floor. Quarterly fire drills.
  • ✓ Pickup verification — children below Class 5 are released only to parents or to authorised persons named in the school records. Photo ID required for new pick-ups.
  • ✓ Background-verified staff — every teaching and non-teaching staff member undergoes police verification before appointment.
