Refund policy

The admission fee is non-refundable once the admission is confirmed. Tuition fees are refundable on a pro-rata basis only if a written withdrawal request is submitted before the end of the first quarter (30 June 2026). After that date, fees for the current academic year are not refundable. The annual charges and transport fees are non-refundable from the start of the academic session.

⚠️ Disclaimer: Fees are subject to annual revision. Please confirm the current fee structure with the school office at the time of admission.

Concessions

  • ✦ Sibling concession: 10% on tuition fee for the second child, 15% for the third child onwards.
  • ✦ RTE quota: 25% of seats in LKG and Class 1 are reserved under the Right to Education Act. No fee is charged for these seats.
  • ✦ Merit concession: Students securing 90%+ in the previous year's board examinations are eligible for a 25% concession on tuition fees for Class 11 and 12.
