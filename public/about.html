About us · Est. 2008

A small school, in a small town, with very big care.

For nearly twenty years, families from Virat Nagar, Kotputli and the wider Behror region have trusted us with their children.

Our story

Where it began

Two rooms, twenty-four students, two teachers. The families of Virat Nagar needed a serious English-medium school close to home — and a small group of educators decided to build one.

Where we are

Today we teach more than twelve hundred students from LKG to Class 12, on a real campus with smart classrooms, science labs, a library, a playground, and our own fleet of buses.

What we're proud of

Strong board results, year after year. But the thing we're most proud of is harder to measure: that our students leave us a little more kind, a little more confident, and a little more curious than when they arrived.

What we believe

Small is good

Eighteen students in a class. Teachers who know each child by name.

Foundations matter

Strong reading, clear writing, real arithmetic. Everything else is built on these.

Discipline, not fear

Routines, expectations, follow-through. The point of discipline is freedom.

Parents are partners

We keep parents close, communicate often, and trust them.

From the principal

"Every child who walks in is someone's whole world. We hold that trust carefully."
