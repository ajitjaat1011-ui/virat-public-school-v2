Our History

Two decades of service to the Kotputli community

Virat Public School was founded in 2008 by a small group of educators and community members who saw a need for a serious, well-run English-medium school in the Virat Nagar area of Kotputli. At the time, families who wanted their children to study in English had limited options, and many promising students were forced to commute long distances or leave the area altogether.

The school opened its doors in a modest two-room building with 24 students and two teachers. Within five years, the school had outgrown its original premises twice and moved to the present campus on the Kotputli–Behror road. In 2012, the school received its first affiliation from the Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education, and the first batch of Class 10 students sat for their board examinations in 2014.

Today, the school serves more than 1,200 students from LKG through Class 12 across science, commerce, and arts streams. The campus has grown to include smart classrooms, science and computer laboratories, a library, a spacious playground, and a fleet of transport buses. The school has been recognized by RBSE for consecutive years of strong board results.

None of this would have been possible without the quiet trust of the families who chose us, year after year, and the steady work of a faculty that takes seriously the responsibility entrusted to it.

What guides us

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a school where children of Virat Nagar and the surrounding villages receive an education that prepares them not only for examinations, but for a life of clear thinking, quiet confidence, and genuine contribution to their community.

Our Mission

  • ✦ To provide a structured, well-taught curriculum that builds strong foundations in language, mathematics, science, and the humanities.
  • ✦ To keep class sizes small enough that every teacher knows every child by name, by habit, and by need.
  • ✦ To treat parents as partners in the work of raising their children, communicating clearly and frequently.
  • ✦ To instil the values of discipline, kindness, and hard work that have always been the bedrock of strong communities.
  • ✦ To make our school a place children are happy to come to each morning — and a place parents are proud to send them.
Our values

The principles we hold to

Integrity

We say what we mean, and we do what we say. Our word is our bond with parents, students, and staff.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to high standards in teaching, in discipline, and in the everyday care of the school.

Compassion

We see every child as a person first. Mistakes are part of growing; kindness is not optional.

Curiosity

We want our students to ask good questions, to wonder, and to keep on learning long after they leave us.

Principal's Message

"Every child who walks in is someone's whole world."

Dear Parents and Students,

It is my privilege to welcome you to Virat Public School. For nearly two decades, our school has served the families of this region with a simple promise — that we will take your child's education seriously, treat them with respect, and prepare them well for the life ahead.

Education, as I have come to understand it, is not only about marks and ranks. It is about helping a young person become steady, kind, and capable. It is about teaching them to read carefully, to think for themselves, to speak clearly, and to work with others. It is about the small everyday habits that, over twelve years, add up to a confident, useful adult.

I warmly invite prospective parents to visit our campus, see our classrooms, meet our teachers, and decide for yourselves whether this is the right place for your child. We have been blessed with the trust of over a thousand families, and we do not take that trust lightly.

With warm regards,
Dr. Meera Singh
Principal, Virat Public School
