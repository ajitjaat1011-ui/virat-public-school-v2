Admissions Open for Academic Year 2026-27

Online applications are now being accepted for LKG through Class 11.

  1. Home
  2. Notices
  3. Admissions Open 2026-27
Admission Published: 15 July 2026 · By: Office of the Principal

Dear Parents and Students,

We are pleased to announce that Virat Public School is now accepting applications for admission to the academic year 2026-27, across classes from LKG to Class 11 (Science, Commerce, and Arts streams).

Available seats

  • LKG — 60 seats
  • UKG — 40 seats
  • Class 1 to 8 — limited seats in select classes
  • Class 9 — 30 seats (based on previous academic record)
  • Class 11 (Science) — 40 seats
  • Class 11 (Commerce) — 30 seats
  • Class 11 (Arts) — 20 seats

How to apply

The admission process is fully online. Please follow these steps:

  1. Visit the Admissions page on our website.
  2. Fill out the inquiry form with accurate details.
  3. Our office will contact you within one working day.
  4. Visit the campus with your child for a friendly interaction.
  5. Submit documents and complete fee payment to confirm admission.

Age criteria

As per RBSE norms, the minimum age as on 31 March 2026:

  • LKG — 3 years 6 months
  • UKG — 4 years 6 months
  • Class 1 — 5 years 6 months

Important dates

  • Application window: 15 July 2026 – 15 August 2026
  • Campus interactions: From 20 July 2026 onwards
  • Admission confirmation deadline: 30 August 2026
  • Session commences: 1 April 2027

Documents required

Please have the following ready for the campus visit:

  • Birth certificate of the child (original + 2 photocopies)
  • Aadhaar card of student and parents (photocopies)
  • Transfer certificate (for Class 2 and above)
  • Previous year report card (for Class 2 and above)
  • 4 recent passport-size photographs of the student
  • Address proof

For any queries, please call the school office at +91 90000 00000 between 9:00 AM and 4:00 PM, Monday to Saturday.

We look forward to welcoming your family to the Virat Public School community.

Warm regards,
Office of the Principal
Virat Public School, Kotputli
