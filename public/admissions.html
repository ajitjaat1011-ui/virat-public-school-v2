What to bring for admission

Original documents are required for verification at the time of admission. Photocopies will be retained by the school.

  • ✓ Birth Certificate (original + 2 photocopies)
  • ✓ Aadhaar Card of student and both parents (photocopy only)
  • ✓ Transfer Certificate (for Class 2 and above)
  • ✓ Previous year's Report Card (for Class 2 and above)
  • ✓ 4 recent passport-size photographs of the student
  • ✓ 2 photographs each of both parents
  • ✓ Address proof (electricity bill, rent agreement, or similar)
  • ✓ Caste / Income Certificate (only for RTE quota applicants)

Common questions

Frequently asked questions

What is the medium of instruction? +

The medium of instruction is English. Hindi is a compulsory second language, and Sanskrit is offered as a third language from Class 6 to 8.

Does the school provide transport? +

Yes. The school operates a fleet of GPS-tracked buses covering Virat Nagar, Kotputli town, Behror, and surrounding villages. Transport is optional and charged separately.

Is there an entrance test? +

There is no formal entrance test for LKG to Class 8. For Class 9 and 11, admission is based on the previous academic record and a short interaction with the principal.

What is the student-teacher ratio? +

We maintain an average student-teacher ratio of 18:1 to ensure personal attention for every child.

Are there any concessions for RTE quota? +

Yes. 25% of seats in LKG and Class 1 are reserved under the Right to Education Act. Please mention this in your inquiry and our office will guide you on the documentation required.
